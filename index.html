Justin de la Cruz

Hi! I'm a librarian and musician living in Asheville, NC. I've worked in public, academic, and medical libraries on digital literacy.

 

Work

See my full Curriculum Vitae.

I've been working in public and academic libraries since 2011. I mainly manage technology labs and focus on digital literacy and educational technology. This means I answer questions and provide training on lots of different technology topics. Lately, I've been working on developing data science educational resources. Podcast
