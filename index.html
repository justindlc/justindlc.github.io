Justin de la Cruz

Hi! I'm a librarian and musician living in Asheville, NC. I've worked in public, academic, and medical libraries, mostly teaching about technology.

 

Work

I've been working in libraries since 2011. I'm broadly interested in education — how people learn and share information. I've taught a variety of topics about technology, including web development, productivity, tech hygiene, and ethical issues. In my current role I help teach librarians about topics in data services (e.g., Python coding, data management).
